Company Overview

ThreadCraft Textiles is a garment manufacturer and retailer with factories in Tirupur and Surat, and retail outlets in 15 cities. The company produces men's, women's, and kids' apparel under its own brand and as private labels for international brands.

Operations management has identified issues with excess fabric waste, production delays, and inconsistent export order fulfillment. IBM Cognos Analytics is now being used to monitor production efficiency, sales, and export compliance.

📦 Dataset Available
  • Product Code, Garment Type, Size, Color, Fabric Type, Factory Location
  • Production Date, Units Planned, Units Produced, Units Rejected, Fabric Used (meters), Fabric Wasted
  • Order Type (Domestic/Export), Buyer Name, Order Quantity, Delivered Quantity, Delivery Date
  • Retail Outlet City, Units Sold, MRP, Selling Price, Revenue, Season (Summer/Winter/Festival)
